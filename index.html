What You'll Need

For Real Ball Mode:

  • Meta Quest 3 or Quest 3S headset
  • A real putter and golf ball (solid white or colored ball works best)
  • At least 2 ft of putting surface (putting mat or carpet)
  • A backstop or cushion to prevent the ball rolling away
  • Good lighting for consistent tracking
Note: The putting surface should contrast with the ball. A white ball on a darker colored surface works well. The tracking area (1x2ft) should be free of lines/markings of similar color to the ball.

You can also play using Quest Touch Controllers, but the full magic is in real ball mode.
